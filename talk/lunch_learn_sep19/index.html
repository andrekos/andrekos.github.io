Abstract

For this Friday’s Lunch & Learn session, we’ll briefly go through some of the more poplar definitions of a data scientist, compare data scientists to data analysts and machine learning engineers in terms of skills, responsibilities etc. I will briefly mention Kaggle as the best platform for all three to share their skills and learn new tricks of the trade. We’ll then briefly explore some of the recent SOTA tools, namely ULMFit for NLP and EffieintNets for CV, both theory and practice. We will conclude with a Q&A.
