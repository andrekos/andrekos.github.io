Dr Andrey Kostenko

Hi, I’m Andrey.

I work and live in Perth, Australia, the country’s sunniest capital city. Before joining Western Power as Forecasting Analyst, I worked for them as a contractor advising on the more technical bits of predictive modelling and forecasting. Before that, I worked as Data Analyst for the Alice Solar City project, in Alice Springs, the country’s sunniest town. In Australia, I have earned a PhD in business statistics and econometrics from Monash University, where I studied time series modelling and forecasting with Rob J Hyndman and other faculty members. By then I have already had an MBA from the UK (thanks to the scholarship of the President of the Russian Federation to study overseas) and my first university degree from Russia (thanks to my parents).

In addition to my ongoing interest in all sorts of data-driven analytics, econometrics and business statistics in general, and time series modelling and forecasting in particular,

over the past ten years I have been interested in many things including:

  • R, RStudio | SAS STAT/ETS/IML | Python, Pandas and friends for data analysis;
  • SQL | Oracle PL/SQL | Microsoft T-SQL and firends for data extraction;
  • LaTeX | JabRef | Pandoc | Markdown and friends for formal academic writing;
  • HTML5 | CSS3 | JS | Jekyll and friends for non-academic writings and other fun.

I am sure I forgot something big and important in the list above, created in no particular order, but it should give one an idea of more technical things I am interested in and thus more or less familiar with. In general, I like learning new tools and tricks, even where I could readily use my old ones.
